            Franchise Application Setup

            Phase 01

            Ask Our Team For "pro Setup" To Know And Then Space Or Shop Inspection Done By Franchies

            Phase 02

            Franchise Booking to be done with a deposit of Rs. 1,00,000 in NOTICA LLP Account & Application Form Following which the agreement and Legal Copies are Completed and got notarized.

            Phase 03

            1st Installment of Rs.3,50,000 the Franchise to be paid within 10 Days of the Booking to prevent lapse of franchise bookin

            Phase 04

            All Materials are dispatched against the Balance Full Payment (2,49,999/-)following. Chef Training is Done with Standard Operations Procedures (SOP).

            Phase 05

            Agreement period starts from the date of Inauguration & monthly monitoring with helpline executive assigned.
